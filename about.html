About

Hi! My name is Samira Said (or Sam) and I like to think of my (ongoing) design journey to be like a pin ball machine -- I am always on the move, touching different points of creativity to get new perspectives and refine my design thinking. Some of those points have ranged from interning as a web applications intern to minoring in digital culture. When I’m not doing things to help me get my degree, I like to take draw, make photography, and avidly eat food.
